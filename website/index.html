About application

The application is designed to monitor different water and saltwater pools. Measurement of temperature and water level as well as the condition of living organisms in it. In an emergency, the application notifies the authorities as well as rescuers and veterinarians.

Аbout body

Our current idea is a beast with a diameter of 50 centimeters that is placed just below the surface of the water to wet through 360 city cameras to monitor the seabed and its inhabitants. There is a person who is constantly watching the cameras, but the artificial one is also important. In the event of an emergency, people in the area receive a notification on their phones, and if the body is located deep in the sea or ocean, teams are notified.
